Our Story

Caramel Foods & Beverages started as a small craft kitchen in the heart of Kampala. We believed that every person deserves a warm, delicious moment in their day. Whether it's a perfectly drizzled latte or a freshly baked croissant straight from the oven.

Today, we serve hundreds of happy customers daily, and our passion for quality has never wavered.

Who Are We

Caramel Foods and Beverages is a craft kitchen where golden moments are made, from the aroma of freshly baked pastries, both junk and healthy meals to the smooth, rich warmth of a perfectly drizzled latte and soft-relaxing drinks.

Every recipe we create is tested with care so that what reaches your table is nothing short of perfect.

Some of the foods prepared are pastries,croissants,pies,cookies, shortbread and fastfoods

The beverages surved are classic coffee,tea,creafted drinks,cappuccinos,warm milk,lattes and cozy drinks
